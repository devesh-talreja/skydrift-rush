# 🌩️ SkyDrift Rush - Ultimate Racing Survival

[![Game Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-2.0-brightgreen)]()   [![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-stable-blue)]()   [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-web-lightgrey)]()

**SkyDrift Rush** is an adrenaline-pumping racing survival game where you control a futuristic vehicle through increasingly chaotic highways.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview

SkyDrift Rush challenges you to survive against waves of obstacles while managing limited resources and unlocking powerful upgrades across 26 intense levels.

### Core Features

- **🚗 7 Unique Vehicles** - Unlock distinct cars through progression.
- **🏁 26 Challenging Levels** - Progressive difficulty with unique tracks.
- **🕊️ Flying Mechanic** - Unique ability to lift your car over obstacles.
- **⚡ 6 Power-up Types** - Strategic power-ups including shield, speed boost, and chaos.
- **♾️ Game Modes** - Campaign, Unlimited, and Legendary survival modes.
- **🏆 Achievement System** - Special recognition and rewards for completion.

---

## 🎯 How to Play

### Basic Controls

| Action      | Key                 |
| ----------- | ------------------- |
| Steer Left  | ⬅️ or A           |
| Steer Right | ➡️ or D           |
| Brake       | ⬇️ or S           |
| Fly         | ⬆️ (tap) or SPACE |
| Pause       | P                   |

*Note: Acceleration is automatic.*

### Flying Mechanic

- **Lifting Off:** Avoid ground obstacles by smoothly ascending your vehicle over them.
- **Limited Charges:** You start with 2 fly charges (max 4).
- **Replenish:** Recover charges via fly power-ups or by navigating 3 wide obstacles successfully.

## ⚡ Power-ups

- **❤️ Repair** - Restores durability.
- **🕊️ Fly Charge** - Adds 1 fly token.
- **🚀 Speed Boost** - Temporary speed increase.
- **🛡️ Shield** - Short invincibility.
- **⚠️ Chaos** - Increases obstacle and power-up frequency.
- **✨ Clear** - Removes all on-screen obstacles.

*Bonus Update: Every 3 wide obstacles successfully navigated grants +1 fly token!*

---

## 🏆 Game Modes

- **Campaign (26 Levels)**: Progress through increasingly difficult challenges, ending in the "IMPOSSIBLE" rank.
- **♾️ Unlimited Mode**: Endless survival with gradually increasing speed.
- **👑 Legendary Mode**: Extended intense challenge with higher difficulty, starting at a faster base pace.

## 🏅 Achievement System

- **Legendary Driver Achievement:** Overcome all 26 campaign levels to earn a permanent badge and unlock a special secret car!

## 🖥️ System Requirements & Game Tips

- **Requirements:** Any modern web browser with JavaScript support.
- **Tips:** Master the fly timing, preserve your hearts (3 hits maximum), stay clear of the walls (which stun you for 2s), and chain power-ups defensively in the later rounds!

## 🚀 Game Balance & Technical Features

- SkyDrift Rush leverages local storage to autosave progress, unlocking cars and keeping records effortlessly.
- Enjoy dynamic difficulty curves from forgiving early courses to punishing late-game stages.
- The game engine natively uses Canvas rendering for smooth 60 FPS gameplay, delivering clear UI overlays and impactful visuals like screen shudders and dynamic collision particles.
- Use your power-ups (like Chaos + Shields) to clear unpredictable traffic patterns!
